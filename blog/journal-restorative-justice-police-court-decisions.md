## Why This Matters

The restorative justice approach offers a more humane and effective solution for various minor criminal offenses. Peaceful settlements between offenders and victims can produce better outcomes compared to lengthy and costly conventional judicial processes.

This paper provides a comprehensive legal framework for understanding how police-mediated peace agreements can be transformed into enforceable court decisions with permanent legal force.
